Industries - 2015

Politicians may have a personal stake in the success of the industries they regulate. Having a checking account at a bank isn't considered an investment in the commercial banking industry; owning stock in the bank, however, is.

This table displays the industries in which public officials have investments.

(Note: Politicians are only required to disclose the value range of their investments, so we provide the minimum and maximum value of investments by industry. For example, if three members each report holding Microsoft stock worth between $1,001 and $5,000, the total Microsoft holding would be listed as $3,003 to $15,000 in the computer software industry.)
