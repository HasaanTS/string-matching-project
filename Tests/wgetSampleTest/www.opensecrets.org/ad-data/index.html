Cataloging FCC Filings

This page contains filings submitted to the Federal Communications Commission by broadcasters around the country. Most radio, TV, satellite and cable providers are required to submit a "political file" to the FCC. The documents they contain allow the public to track each station's contracts, correspondence and other filings as they pertain to the candidates, super PACs, dark money groups and other organizations running political and issue ads. CRP is now downloading, parsing, and updating this data on a daily basis and will begin to make parts of it available as time and resources permit.
