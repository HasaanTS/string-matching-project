Net Worth - 2015

It is difficult to gauge what a lawmaker is worth because disclosure forms do not require exact values. Instead, lawmakers report the value of assets and liabilities within a range.

For example, Energy Secretary Rick Perry said the value of his San Antonio rental property was between $100,001 and $250,000 in 2016. This does provide a minimum, maximum and average value ($175,000) of the asset, however.

To calculate net worth, therefore, we added a lawmaker's ranges of assets and subtracted their range of liabilities. Next, we calculated the midpoint of the resulting range and used this figure for the rankings below.
(Read more about our methodology.)
